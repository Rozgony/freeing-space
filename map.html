FREEING SPACE

About

Neoliberal Capitalism is a system of governance in which corporations work hand in hand with the state to colonize every corner of our physical and mental universe in the pursuit of profit. In this system, land is not a territory but a collection of parcels from which to extract rent and resources, and we are not humans but Human Capital. While some would claim there is no outside of Capitalism, the purpose of this map is to vizualize precisely the attempts to build that outside.

    The spaces on this map are:
  • Not profit-driven
  • Non-state
  • Democratically operated
  • Non-exclusive on grounds of identity

Our hope is that collecting these heterogeneous efforts on a single map aids the construction of worlds outside of state and capital.
